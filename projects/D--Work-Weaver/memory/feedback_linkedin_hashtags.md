## Feedback: LinkedIn Posts Must Include Hashtags

### The Rule
Every LinkedIn post must end with 8-10 relevant hashtags. Hashtags improve discoverability and LinkedIn algorithm performance.

### When This Applies
- All LinkedIn posts (standalone posts, carousel captions, etc.)
- Every single post that goes to LinkedIn

### How to Apply
1. Write the LinkedIn post content (150-200 words)
2. Add 1-2 line breaks after content
3. List 8-10 hashtags separated by spaces
4. Hashtags should reflect core topics from the post

### Example Format
```
[LinkedIn post content]

#ProductStrategy #Orchestration #Technology #BrowserWars #Competition #Strategy #ProductLeadership #MarketDominance #TechHistory #Innovation
```

### Why This Matters
- LinkedIn algorithm prioritizes posts with hashtags
- Hashtags increase reach and engagement
- Improves content discoverability for target audience
- Professional posts include hashtags

### Don't Apply These Rules
- Twitter (has different hashtag strategy)
- Blog posts (no hashtags)
- Email content (no hashtags)

### Verification
Before publishing any LinkedIn post, verify:
- ✓ Post content is 150-200 words
- ✓ Hashtags are present at the end
- ✓ 8-10 hashtags included
- ✓ Hashtags are relevant to post topic
- ✓ Hashtags are separated by spaces (no commas)
