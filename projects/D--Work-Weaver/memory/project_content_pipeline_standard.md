### The One-Block Principle
Every raw input (transcript, recording, conversation, idea) produces:
- 1 cornerstone blog post (2,000-3,000 words)
- 3-4 LinkedIn posts (distillations of blog sections)
- 2-3 Twitter threads (further distillations)
- This is ALL. No volume for volume's sake.

### Content Pipeline Trigger Words
