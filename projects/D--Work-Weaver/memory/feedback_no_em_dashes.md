## Rule: Em-Dashes Are Banned in All Content Output

**Never use:** — (em-dash)

**Why:** Em-dashes are a known AI writing fingerprint. Overuse breaks reading rhythm and marks content as machine-generated.

## Replacements

| Instead of | Use |
|---|---|
| "The product — built on memory — gets smarter." | "The product is built on memory. It gets smarter." |
| "Distribution wins — every time." | "Distribution wins. Every time." |
| "Engineering gets you in the game — distribution wins it." | "Engineering gets you in the game. Distribution wins it." |
| "Facebook paid $1B — not for the code." | "Facebook paid $1B. Not for the code." |

## Applies To
- Blog posts
- LinkedIn posts
- Twitter/X threads
- Email copy
- Scripts and voiceovers
- Any user-facing content output
