## Non-Negotiable Rules for Every Content Piece

### 1. One Deep Block Over Many Shallow Posts
- Choose the SINGLE strongest signal from raw input
- Apply all skills and agents to that one signal
- Do NOT generate 40 pieces from one source — generate 1 cornerstone + 3-4 distillations

### 2. Every Statement Must Have a Verified, Relevant Example
- Not "companies that use AI grow faster" → cite Instagram ($1B, 13 people, 30M users)
- Not "distribution matters" → cite Standard Oil's pipeline control by 1879 (90% US refinery)
- Example must be STRUCTURALLY relevant (same mechanism), not just topically adjacent
- Weak analogy (surface similarity) is worse than no analogy

### 3. Research Verification (Truth Engine) Runs BEFORE Writing Begins
- Agastya verifies all key claims before a single sentence is drafted
- If a claim cannot be verified, it is modified or removed — not published with uncertainty
- The Matthew Berman story was removed because it was unverifiable; YouTube ($1.65B, Oct 2006) replaced it

### 4. Architecture: Fractal Loops (Master Blog Post Skill)
- 1/3/1 rhythm: short punch → 3-sentence development → short closer
- Each section ends with an information gap that pulls into the next section
- No walls of text — every paragraph earns its place
- Slippery slope: reader cannot stop at any section

### 5. Fewer Words, More Depth
- Target: 2,500 words with examples and evidence = more impactful than 4,000 words without
- Every sentence tested: "Does this add value or move the reader forward? If not, delete it."
- No filler, no AI fluff, no hedging phrases

### 6. Historical Analogies with Structural Mechanism
- Gutenberg/Manutius: technology diffuses, distribution compounds (same mechanism as Instagram/WhatsApp)
- Standard Oil: pipelines = distribution infrastructure (same mechanism as behavioral memory)
- Mycelium networks: compounding relational depth (biological twin to memory moat)
- Each analogy must have the SAME underlying mechanism — not just surface resemblance

### 7. The Signal Refinery Pipeline Sequence
Must run in order: The Razor → Truth Engine → Lateral Thinker → Reality OS → Strategist → Write
- Do NOT skip verification phases to write faster
- Research gate must be approved before writing begins

### 8. Voice
- CEO's perspective, not AI narrator
- Direct quotes from raw source material woven in as authentic voice
- Contractions in conversational sections
- No corporate jargon, no AI detection triggers
