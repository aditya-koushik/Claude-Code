# Eval Criteria: content_formatter_linkedin

## Scoring Rubric

| # | Criterion | Weight | Description |
|---|-----------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | Hashtag Compliance | 20% | Exactly 8-10 hashtags on the final line. Space-separated. No commas. No hashtags in body text. |
| 2 | Hook Placement | 20% | Strongest/most compelling sentence is on line 1. Hook stops the scroll. Uses pattern interrupt, bold claim, number, or question. |
| 3 | Mobile Formatting | 20% | Blank line after every 1-3 sentences. No paragraph longer than 3 sentences. White space between all sections. |
| 4 | Bold Usage | 15% | 2-4 key phrases bolded with double asterisks. No full sentences bolded. Bold highlights the main takeaway, stat, or framework name. |
| 5 | CTA Presence | 15% | Ends body with one clear CTA. Prefers engagement questions ("What's your take?"). No multiple competing CTAs. |
| 6 | Word Count | 10% | Total word count between 150-300 words (excluding hashtags). |

**Total: 100%**

## Scoring Scale
- 0-2: Major formatting violations or missing element
- 3-4: Present but poorly executed
- 5-6: Adequate but not optimized
- 7-8: Well formatted with minor issues
- 9-10: Perfect LinkedIn formatting

## Pass Threshold
**Composite score >= 8.0/10**

## Automatic Fail Conditions
- Fewer than 8 or more than 10 hashtags = automatic fail
- Hashtags appear inside body text (not at end) = automatic fail
- Commas between hashtags = automatic fail
- No hook on line 1 (starts with filler/context) = automatic fail
- Word count below 100 or above 400 = automatic fail
