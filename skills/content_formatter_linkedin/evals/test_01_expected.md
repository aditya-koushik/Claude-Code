# Expected Output Characteristics: Test 01

## Must Exhibit

1. **Hook on line 1:** "The system of record is dying." or a stronger rewrite appears as the very first line, standalone. Must be attention-grabbing and scroll-stopping.
2. **Blank lines between paragraphs:** Every 1-3 sentences separated by a blank line. No wall of text.
3. **No paragraph exceeds 3 sentences:** Every block of text has 1-3 sentences max.
4. **2-4 bolded phrases:** Key terms like "orchestration layer", "63%", "47 SaaS tools", or "SaaS tax" bolded. Not full sentences.
5. **Exactly 8-10 hashtags on final line:** Space-separated. No commas. Mix of broad (#AI #Enterprise) and niche (#SaaSTools #SystemOfRecord).
6. **No hashtags in body text:** All hashtags appear only on the final line.
7. **CTA at end of body (before hashtags):** An engagement question like "What's the first SaaS tool you'd cut?" or "Is your company ready?"
8. **Word count 150-300:** Content is not truncated or padded.
9. **Content preserved:** All key facts (Infosys, 47 to 12 tools, 63% drop) remain in the output. Formatter does not remove substantive content.

## Must NOT Exhibit

1. No hashtags with commas between them (#AI, #Enterprise)
2. No paragraphs longer than 3 sentences
3. No missing blank lines between sections
4. No more than 4 bolded phrases
5. No full sentences in bold
6. No multiple CTAs
7. No hashtags embedded in body text
