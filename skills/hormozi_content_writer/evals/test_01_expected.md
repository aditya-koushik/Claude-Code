# Expected Output Characteristics: Test 01

## Must Exhibit

1. **Dream Outcome in first 2 sentences:** Must reference cost reduction or call handling automation as a specific outcome (not "AI voice platform" as a feature).
2. **At least 2 specific numbers from the brief:** e.g., "67%", "90 days", "2 hours", "80%", "12 enterprise customers". Not rounded or vague.
3. **Proof stacking with 2+ types:** Must combine at least two of: customer result (67% cost reduction), adoption metric (12 customers), performance stat (99.2% completion rate).
4. **Time delay addressed:** Must mention speed — "90 days", "2 hours setup", or similar. Reader must know when results start.
5. **Effort minimization:** Must mention ease — "no code", "2 hours", "works with existing". Reader must know what they DON'T have to do.
6. **Exactly one CTA:** Must end with "Book a 15-minute demo" or equivalent single action pointing to bitfoundry.ai/demo.
7. **Natural prose flow:** Content reads as a persuasive narrative, not a numbered list of value equation components.
8. **Word count:** 150-300 words.

## Must NOT Exhibit

1. No banned words (delve, dynamic, unlock, revolutionize, harness, comprehensive, landscape, paradigm, synergy, leverage, might, perhaps, arguably)
2. No em-dashes
3. No hedging language ("could potentially", "it's possible that")
4. No multiple CTAs or options ("you can also...", "alternatively...")
5. No feature-first language without tying to outcomes
6. No generic proof ("many companies trust us", "industry-leading")
