# Eval Criteria: hormozi_content_writer

## Scoring Rubric

| # | Criterion | Weight | Description |
|---|-----------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | Dream Outcome Presence | 20% | First 2 sentences state a specific, measurable outcome the audience wants. Must be an outcome, not a feature. |
| 2 | Proof Stacking | 20% | Contains at least 2 distinct proof types (numbers, case studies, testimonials, logos, before/after). Specific numbers required. |
| 3 | Time/Effort Minimization | 15% | Explicitly addresses speed-to-result AND ease of adoption. Must include at least one specific timeframe. |
| 4 | CTA Clarity | 15% | Ends with exactly ONE clear next step. No multiple options. Action verb + specific destination. |
| 5 | Value Equation Flow | 10% | Content flows as natural prose following Dream Outcome > Proof > Speed > Ease > Urgency > CTA. Not a visible checklist. |
| 6 | Voice Compliance | 10% | Matches Bit Foundry AI voice: direct, declarative, no hedging. Short sentences. Enterprise lens. |
| 7 | Banned Word Check | 10% | Zero instances of banned words: delve, dynamic, unlock, revolutionize, harness, comprehensive, landscape, paradigm, synergy, leverage, might, perhaps, arguably. Zero em-dashes. |

**Total: 100%**

## Scoring Scale
- 0-2: Missing entirely or fundamentally wrong
- 3-4: Present but vague or generic
- 5-6: Adequate but not compelling
- 7-8: Strong execution with minor gaps
- 9-10: Excellent, specific, and persuasive

## Pass Threshold
**Composite score >= 8.0/10**

## Automatic Fail Conditions
- Contains any banned word = automatic fail regardless of composite score
- No dream outcome in first 3 sentences = automatic fail
- No CTA present = automatic fail
- Contains em-dash (—) = automatic fail
- Content reads as a bullet-point checklist rather than flowing prose = automatic fail
