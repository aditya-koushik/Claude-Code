# LinkedIn Post Generator 2026

## Purpose
Generates LinkedIn posts built for the 2026 algorithm. The algorithm now rewards meaningful comments over reactions, original content over reshares, expertise signals, and dwell time. Every post uses hook-context-value structure designed to hold attention and drive real conversation.

## When to Use
- When creating original LinkedIn posts from scratch
- When adapting content specifically for LinkedIn's 2026 algorithm
- When the goal is reach, comments, and audience growth on LinkedIn
- As the primary LinkedIn content generation skill in the daily pipeline

## Workflow

### Step 1: Identify the Topic Angle
Pick a specific angle, not a broad topic. "How we cut onboarding from 3 weeks to 3 days using AI" beats "AI in onboarding." The angle should connect to one of the 10 mental models (Simplicity Tax, Speed of Trust, AI as Co-Founder, etc.).

### Step 2: Write the Hook (Lines 1-2)
The first 2 lines determine 80% of engagement. They appear before the "see more" fold. Use one of these formats:
- Pattern interrupt: "Most AI advice is wrong. Here's why."
- Specific number: "We saved $240K last year with one decision."
- Bold question: "What if your entire sales team could be 3 people?"
Write for mobile. Short lines. Big impact.

### Step 3: Provide Context (Why This Matters Now)
3-5 sentences explaining why this topic matters right now. Connect to a trend, a shift, or a personal experience. This section builds credibility and keeps readers scrolling.

### Step 4: Deliver Value (Framework, Insight, or Takeaway)
The core of the post. Give the reader something they can use today. A framework, a decision matrix, a lesson, or a specific insight. Name your frameworks. Use numbered lists when helpful. Be concrete.

### Step 5: CTA (Drive Comments)
End with a question that invites expertise-sharing. Not "agree?" but "What's worked for you?" or "Which of these resonated most?" The 2026 algorithm rewards comments that are 50+ characters. Ask questions that require thoughtful answers.

### Step 6: Add 8-10 Hashtags
Final line. 8-10 hashtags. Space-separated. No commas. Mix broad reach tags (#AI #Leadership #Business) with niche tags (#EnterpriseAI #BuildInPublic #AIStrategy). No hashtags anywhere else in the post.

## Output Format
```
[Hook - 1-2 lines, max impact]

[Context - why this matters now, 3-5 sentences]

[Value - framework, insight, or actionable takeaway]

[CTA - question that invites thoughtful comments]

#Tag1 #Tag2 #Tag3 #Tag4 #Tag5 #Tag6 #Tag7 #Tag8
```

## Key Principles
- 2026 Algorithm priorities: meaningful comments > reactions, original > reshares, expertise signals, dwell time.
- First 2 lines are your entire marketing budget. Spend time on them.
- Write for mobile. Short paragraphs. White space.
- Ask questions that require expertise to answer. This drives quality comments.
- NO engagement bait. "Like if you agree" kills reach in 2026.
- 8-10 hashtags REQUIRED at end. Non-negotiable.
- Mental models: Value Density, Build in Public, Platform Thinking.
