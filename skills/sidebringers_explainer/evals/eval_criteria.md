# Eval Criteria: sidebringers_explainer

## Scoring Rubric

| # | Criterion | Weight | Description |
|---|-----------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | Simplification Accuracy | 25% | Technical concept correctly simplified without losing essential meaning. No factual errors introduced by simplification. |
| 2 | Analogy Quality | 20% | Uses at least 1 concrete analogy that maps accurately to the technical concept. Analogy is accessible to non-technical audience. |
| 3 | Business Impact Framing | 20% | Explains WHY this matters to business leaders. Ties technical concept to revenue, cost, risk, or competitive advantage. |
| 4 | Jargon Elimination | 15% | Zero unexplained technical jargon in output. Every technical term either replaced with plain language or explained in parentheses. |
| 5 | Reading Level | 10% | Output at Grade 5-7 reading level. A non-technical CEO can understand every sentence on first read. |
| 6 | Sidebrinders Framework Applied | 10% | Follows the 7-step framework: Identify concept, Find analogy, Map to business impact, Sector analysis, Employment analysis, Simplify language, Deliver. |

**Total: 100%**

## Pass Threshold
**Composite score >= 8.0/10**

## Automatic Fail Conditions
- Technical error introduced by simplification (wrong analogy that misleads) = automatic fail
- Output still requires technical knowledge to understand = automatic fail
- No analogy used = automatic fail
- No business impact mentioned = automatic fail
- Output exceeds 500 words (simplification should be concise) = automatic fail
