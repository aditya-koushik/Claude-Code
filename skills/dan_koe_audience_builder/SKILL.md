# Dan Koe Audience Builder

## Purpose
Creates content that builds a loyal audience by teaching from personal experience, giving away actionable value, and inviting real engagement. The approach prioritizes trust and utility over promotion. Community before customers. Teach first, sell later.

## When to Use
- When creating educational or personal brand content
- When the goal is audience growth over direct conversion
- When building trust with a new audience segment
- When sharing lessons learned or frameworks from real experience

## Workflow

### Step 1: Identify the Pain Point
Pick one specific problem your audience faces right now. Not a vague category. A real, felt pain. "I don't know which AI tools to pick" beats "AI is confusing." Get specific.

### Step 2: Share Personal Experience
Tell a short story from your own journey. What happened? What did you try? What failed? What worked? Use "I" not "we." Include a specific number or outcome. "I tested 14 AI tools in 30 days" is better than "I tried a lot of tools."

### Step 3: Provide an Actionable Framework
Give your audience something they can use today. A 3-step process. A checklist. A decision matrix. Make it concrete. Name the framework if possible. Named frameworks stick in memory.

### Step 4: Include a Specific Outcome
Show what happens when someone follows your advice. Use numbers. "This saved our team 12 hours per week" or "Revenue went from $40K to $120K in 6 months." Specifics build trust. Vague claims destroy it.

### Step 5: CTA That Invites Engagement
End with a question, a challenge, or an offer of a free resource. Not "follow me for more." Instead: "What's the one AI tool you can't live without? Drop it below." Make them want to respond.

## Output Format
Content structured as: Pain point hook, personal story, framework, specific result, engagement CTA. Each section should flow naturally. Not a listicle. A conversation.

## Key Principles
- Teach from experience, not theory. Your story is your moat.
- Give away your best stuff for free. Generosity builds audiences.
- Build trust through specificity. Vague = forgettable. Specific = credible.
- Community before customers. Serve first, sell later.
- Every post should leave the reader with one thing they can do today.
- Mental models: Build in Public, Value Density, Speed of Trust.
